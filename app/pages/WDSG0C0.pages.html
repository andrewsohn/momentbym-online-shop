PAGE provides a guide for optimization and consistency of Samsung.com pages.
Samsung.com is divided into New Common pages and Old Common pages for production and management.
New Common pages or newly-produced Static pages should comply with the new Common design / publishing / web accessibility / SEO policy. The existing Old Common pages must be produced in compliance with the Old Common design / publishing policy. GNB and Footer are common areas produced under the New Common standard without distinctions.
